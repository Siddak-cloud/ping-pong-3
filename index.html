AI Ping Pong Game







Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move yourself approx 3 to 4 feet away from the laptop
  3. Move your right wrist in ascending and descending manner
  4. A red dot should appear on your write wrist
  5. Press the play button and again move yourself 3 to 4 feet away from laptop
  6. The red paddle is your paddle that will move as per the movement of your right wrist
